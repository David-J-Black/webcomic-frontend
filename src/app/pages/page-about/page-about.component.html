Finding Darwin

Finding Darwin might have begun in concept around a decade ago, with initial designs and concepts surrounding one specific character. The comic has been in development since 2019, went under a pilot phase, and finally had it's first published page in September 2022.

The Author

David Black is an artist turned software developer. They've been drawing all their life, but never saw art as a good career. That being said they love to draw, and as soon as they got out of school they have been putting pen to paper and drawing like crazy.
